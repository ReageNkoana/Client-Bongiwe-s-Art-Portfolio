About Me

Hi, I'm Bongiwe Malepe, I am a visual artist from Eerstegeluk, a small township in Limpopo, South Africa. I am currently residing in Johannesburg, where I am completing my PGCE at the University of Johannesburg . I hold a BA in Visual Arts from the University of Johannesburg, where I majored in painting .My practice includes painting, drawing, and collage, often using acrylic paint ,different ink types , diary pages and shweshwe fabric which is a traditional fabric that women from Southern African groups wear.

I explore memory, family history, cultural identity and stories where women, especially mothers or female leaders are powerful characters who guide and keep families together. My work is shaped by the women in my life , especially my mother, whose stories and resilience continue to guide my practice. Throughout my childhood, bullying at school made me feel isolated and scared . In those moments I found peace in the love and strength of the women and family members who held me up when I felt like giving up.
